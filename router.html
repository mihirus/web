My most mechanically involved creation

I wanted to cut my own longboard decks, so I built this 2.5axis, 3 by 4 foot, rack and pinion driven CNC router. There was nothing I could find short of a industrial machine that was big enough to machine longboard decks. So I designed and built my own.

I love manufacturing. Unfortunately, this thing never cut anything bigger than a small wooden bust of Yoda

I designed the entire thing in Autodesk Inventor(I know, not Solidworks) prior to assembly, using Openbuilds V-slot parts
