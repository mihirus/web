Hyperloop Electric Longboard CNC Router Bamboo Bike Ballbot

My baby.

No remote control required. It knows what you want.

When I built this, there were no electric longboards I knew of that interpreted the rider's intentions like mine did. Only the Zboard came close.

An Arduino and its shield run the feedback loop.I had to account for accidental dismount from the pressure pads, bumps, sensor noise, disconnected wires, and a host of other problems. Now, the only way to fall off is to be kicked off.

The black motor mounts and wheel pulleys are completely 3d printed. The motors provide terrific mid range response, being 2kW each and all.
