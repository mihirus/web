This was the original concept the ionic wind aircraft, my senior capstone project. Scroll down and click for some real demos.

This is a successful *unpowered* glide test. The airplane is fully laden with batteries, thrusters, and power converter. This shows that the airframe side is solid.

The device in the center is the power converter. It converts 180V from the batteries (left) to 40kV out for our dummy 2.9Mohm resistor load. Output power: 525W (sound up!)

If you're wondering what happened - this. We needed a couple more weeks to test our converter with the actual thrusters. In the end, we achieved 120g of thrust at 34kV, before the converter exploded.
