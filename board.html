This is a weight sensing electric longboard. I started designing it in 2014(late 10th grade) and it became functional in 2016. It will accelerate and brake based on the rider's lean, closing the natural control loop that is not harnessed when the rider uses a remote.

When I built this, there were zero electric longboards that interpreted the rider's intentions like mine did. The Zboard came close.

An Arduino and its shield control the motor drivers.I had to account for accidental dismount from the pressure pads, bumps, sensor noise, disconnected wires, and a host of other problems. Right now, the only way to fall off is to be kicked off.

The black motor mounts and wheel pulleys are completely 3d printed. The motors provide terrific mid range response, being 2kW each.
